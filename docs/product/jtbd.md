# JTBD: Just Build with Blocks MVP

Date: 2026-03-05
Repository: Just-Build-Blocks

## Core Job Statement

When I am curious about building with AI but intimidated by technical tooling, I want to upload something familiar and instantly see it transformed into a block-built artifact, so I can feel that creation is accessible and playful again.

## Primary Jobs

1. Turn a known brand mark into a delightful visual proof that "building with GPT and Codex" can feel intuitive.
2. Produce a concept artifact that can seed a stronger video narrative without rebuilding the idea from scratch.

## Job Steps

| Step | Current friction | Desired progress |
|---|---|---|
| Discover | AI creation feels abstract and technical | The experience starts from a familiar upload action |
| Decide | Users do not know what settings or prompts matter | The app chooses a strong default transformation path |
| Execute | Manual art direction would slow the concept down | The logo becomes a block-built still automatically |
| Verify | It is hard to tell if the concept is emotionally working | The result feels shareable, playful, and obviously video-ready |
| Maintain | Future storytelling could require rework | The app exports scene data that can feed a later Remotion piece |

## Outcome Expectations

- Speed: logo to hero still in under a minute.
- Confidence: the user immediately understands the point of the demo without explanation.
- Control: a few simple knobs are available, but the default path still feels magical.

## Forces of Adoption

### Push (pain)

- Coding tools can feel gated behind engineering fluency.

### Pull (benefits)

- The experience makes creation feel playful, visual, and immediate.

### Anxiety (risk)

- The output might look childish, generic, or too inaccurate to take seriously.

### Habits (status quo)

- People default to static brand decks or manual design mockups when they want to communicate ideas.
