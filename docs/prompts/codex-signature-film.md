# Codex Signature Film Spec

## Objective

Create a 45-second product film that crowns the signature-set system after the core model, box, studio, and instructions are already believable.

## Required beats

- Input and anticipation
- Parts sorting
- Early assembly
- Silhouette resolve
- Premium hero reveal
- Studio / instructions crossover
- Keepsake ending

## Camera language

- Precise, tactile, intimate
- Product-reveal pacing, not montage chaos
- Use motion to clarify build phases, not hide weak geometry

## Anti-goals

- No bombastic trailer energy
- No stitched-together demo feel
- No separate film story that ignores the actual model

## Quality bar

- The film proves the same canonical model as the app
- The sacred line lands once and matters
- The ending feels quiet, confident, and memorable

## Acceptance checklist

- Are the phases synchronized with the instruction/studio logic?
- Does the film make the collectible feel real?
- Does the motion amplify clarity instead of compensating for weak structure?
