# Codex Signature Sound Spec

## Objective

Build a sound system that makes the Codex collectible feel tactile, premium, and emotionally complete.

## Required cue families

- Upload / intake
- Sorting
- Snap
- Resolve
- Sacred line
- Quiet possibility
- End settle

## Tone

- Tactile
- Precise
- Intimate
- Calm confidence over spectacle

## Anti-goals

- No copied toy-brand audio memory
- No bombastic trailer hits
- No constant narration

## Quality bar

- Cues reinforce physicality
- Sound supports the object rather than overpowering it
- The sacred line lands once and feels earned

## Acceptance checklist

- Do the cues match build phases?
- Does the soundtrack feel original?
- Would the film still feel premium with the visuals muted and only sound cues playing?
